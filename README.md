# ReLoop

**Love What You Own.**

ReLoop is an AI-powered wardrobe management and circular fashion platform designed to help users maximize the value of the clothing they already own. By combining intelligent wardrobe organization, personalized outfit recommendations, wardrobe analytics, and an integrated marketplace, ReLoop encourages intentional shopping and extends the lifecycle of every garment.

---

## Mission

To make circular fashion effortless by helping every garment stay in the loop longer.

---

## Vision

To become the intelligent operating system for the modern wardrobe, empowering people to manage, style, and circulate their clothing through one seamless platform.

---

## The Problem

Many people own more clothing than they regularly wear. As wardrobes grow, garments are often forgotten, resulting in unnecessary purchases, closet clutter, decision fatigue, and increased textile waste.

Despite having full closets, many still feel like they have "nothing to wear."

---

## The Solution

ReLoop transforms an ordinary closet into an intelligent wardrobe.

Users can digitize their clothing, receive personalized outfit recommendations, understand how they use their wardrobe through analytics, and seamlessly sell, swap, or donate pieces they no longer wear. By combining AI with circular fashion principles, ReLoop helps people get more value from what they already own before purchasing something new.

---

## Core Features

### Digital Closet

* Upload and organize clothing
* Categorize by type, color, season, brand, material, and occasion
* Search and filter wardrobe items
* Save favorite pieces

### AI Stylist

* Generate personalized outfits
* Recommend looks based on weather, occasion, and wardrobe history
* Rediscover unworn clothing
* Save favorite outfit combinations

### Wardrobe Analytics

* Track wear frequency
* Monitor wardrobe utilization
* Calculate cost per wear
* View Closet Health Score
* Identify frequently and infrequently worn items

### Marketplace

* Buy, sell, swap, or donate clothing
* Browse community listings
* Filter by category, size, condition, and price
* Support local and campus-based exchanges

### Smart Shopping (Planned)

* Evaluate potential purchases before buying
* Detect similar items already owned
* Recommend purchases that complement an existing wardrobe
* Encourage intentional consumption
